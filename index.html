Student IT events around Columbia

Bring real computer science and IT courses to schools.

IT-oLody hosts monthly student meetings, where you can become a student ambassador, and learn about what works and doesn't work in schools when it comes to computer science. You'll be able to hang out with like-minded students also interested in improving the state of IT at their schools.
